Astronomy Club

I am the Public Relations of Georgia Tech's Astronomy Club.

I am in charge of running our social medias and outreach. Our socials include twitter, instagram, facebook and LinkedIn. This includes making posters, banners, instragram posts, and taking pictures.


In astronomy club, we go to the obeservatory and use telescopes to view stars, planets, and other astrological bodies. Every first Thursday of the month, we host a public night where we set up telescopes for anyone in the community to view. My favorite part of being in this club is viewing the constellations in the Springtime.
